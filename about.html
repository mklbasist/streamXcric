ℹ️ About Us

Who We Are

streamXcric is a dedicated platform for cricket enthusiasts who want to relive the greatest moments in the sport. We believe every century, every wicket, and every legendary performance deserves to be remembered and shared.

Our Purpose

We curate and organize cricket highlights from across the globe—from historic Test matches to dominant IPL performances. Whether you're looking to revisit an iconic innings or discover legendary cricket moments, streamXcric connects you with the content that matters.

What We Offer

Our platform provides:

  • Player Highlights: Browse centuries and fifties by your favorite cricketers
  • Match Archives: Explore Test cricket matches organized by year and team
  • Cricket News: Stay updated with breaking news and trending topics
  • Advanced Stats: Access detailed player matchup statistics and performance data

Our Commitment

We respect intellectual property rights and work only with authorized, publicly available content. Every highlight on streamXcric is embedded from legitimate sources, and we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of copyright compliance.

For Cricket Fans, By Cricket Fans

streamXcric exists because we understand the passion that drives cricket fans. We're here to make it easier to celebrate, analyze, and share the sport we love.
