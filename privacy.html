🔒 Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

streamXcric ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information: When you visit streamXcric, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Geographic location (general)

This data is collected through standard analytics tools and does not identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience

  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We Do Not Sell Your Data: streamXcric does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking

streamXcric does not set cookies on its own servers. However, embedded videos from YouTube, Dailymotion, and similar platforms may use cookies and tracking technologies in accordance with their privacy policies. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

streamXcric does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of streamXcric constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Page and select "General Feedback."
